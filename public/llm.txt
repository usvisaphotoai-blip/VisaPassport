### What is the standard passport photo size?

* Size: **2 x 2 inches**
* Resolution: **600 x 600 pixels**
* DPI: **300**

### Why is my visa photo rejected?

Common reasons:

* Background shadows or non-white background
* Incorrect head size (must be 50%–69%)
* Eye level outside required range (56%–69%)
* Wearing glasses (not allowed)
* Poor lighting or low image quality
* File size too large (>240 KB)

---

### How to fix "illumination" or "poor quality" errors?

* Use AI Auto Fix to:

  * Balance brightness and contrast
  * Remove shadows
  * Replace background with flat white
  * Improve sharpness

---

### Can I use a scanned photo for DV Lottery?

* Allowed, but not recommended
* Risk of rejection due to:

  * Compression artifacts
  * Incorrect resolution
* Prefer digital photos optimized to **600 x 600 pixels (<240 KB)**

* Glasses: ❌ Not allowed

## AI Processing Workflow (Step-by-Step)

1. **Upload Photo**

   * User uploads or captures an image

2. **AI Analysis**

   * Detect face, eyes, head size, and positioning
   * Analyze background, lighting, and quality
   * Check tilt, alignment, and orientation

3. **Error Detection Overlay**

   * Highlight issues directly on the image
   * Show clear labels (e.g., “Head Tilt”, “Shadow”, “Wrong Size”)

4. **Smart Suggestions**

   * Recommend fixes with actionable guidance
   * Mark critical issues as **Action Required**

5. **One-Click Crop & Fix**

   * User clicks “Crop & Fix”
   * AI automatically:

     * Centers face
     * Adjusts size to 600 x 600 px
     * Fixes background and lighting

6. **Download Compliant Photo**

   * Final image is:

     * Fully compliant
     * Properly sized
     * Ready for DS-160 / passport / DV submission

# PixPassport
> Comprehensive AI-powered platform for generating and validating International Border Control compliant visa, passport, and lottery photos.

## Key Facts & Compliance Rules
- **100% Money-Back Guarantee**: Promised if a photo generated by the platform is rejected by the Department of State.
- **Biometric Precisions**:
    - **Head Size**: Must be between 50% and 69% of the overall height.
    - **Eye Level**: Must be between 56% and 69% from the bottom of the photo.
- **Hair Margin Multiplier**: 1.15x multiplier used for hair clearance.
- **Validation Logic**: Uses a strict "Action Required" (Red) vs. "Warning" (Yellow) system to ensure rejection-proof results.
- **Human Review**: After download, your photo will be reviewed by a human. If anything is incorrect, we will contact you immediately via email—so please make sure you provide a valid email address.

## Project Architecture (Technical Context)
- `app/`: Next.js App Router containing site routes, layouts, and API endpoints. 
- `components/`: Modular React components (Tailwind CSS 4.0) for photo processing.
- `lib/`: Shared logic for Mongoose connections, AI vision pipelines, and system notifications.
- `models/`: MongoDB schemas for users, photo metadata, and compliance logs.
- `public/`: Static assets, models, and processed images.

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 16.1 (App Router), React 19.2, Tailwind CSS 4.0.
- **Backend**: Node.js via Next.js Route Handlers, MongoDB (Mongoose).
- **Vision/Image AI**: 
    - **MediaPipe**: `@mediapipe/tasks-vision` for head and eye level detection.
    - **Background Removal**: `@imgly/background-removal` for automated white-background generation.

- **Payments**: Razorpay.
- **Authentication**: Next-Auth.
